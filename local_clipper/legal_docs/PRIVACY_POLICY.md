## 1. Introduction

Welcome to CustosAI Clipper ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our desktop application and related services.

By using CustosAI Clipper, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Hardware Identifier (HWID)
- **What we collect:** A unique hardware identifier (UUID) from your device
- **Purpose:** License validation, piracy prevention, and ensuring compliance with our single-device licensing model
- **Storage:** Stored securely in Whop.io's infrastructure
- **Retention:** Retained for the duration of your active license plus 30 days

### 2.2 License Information
- **What we collect:** License key, activation date, expiration date
- **Purpose:** Verify license validity and provide access to the software
- **Storage:** Stored securely with our payment processor (Whop.io)

### 2.3 Anonymous Usage Data (Optional)
- **What we collect:** App version, operating system version, crash reports (if you opt-in)
- **Purpose:** Improve software quality and fix bugs
- **Storage:** Stored in our analytics system (anonymous, no HWID linked)

### 2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect
We do **not** collect:
- Video content you process
- Personal files or data from your computer
- Browsing history
- Location data
- Contact information (unless you provide it for support)

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage Location
- HWID and license data are stored in the United States via Whop.io
- Analytics data is processed in the United States

### 4.2 Security Measures
- All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
- HWID is stored using industry-standard encryption
- We never store your license key in plain text

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

### 5.1 Right to Access
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

### 5.2 Right to Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)
You can request deletion of your HWID and license data by:
1. Using the "Revoke License" feature in the app (Settings → License → Revoke)
2. Emailing us at support@custosai.com

### 5.3 Right to Portability
You can transfer your license to a new device once every 30 days through the app.

### 5.4 Right to Object
You can object to the collection of anonymous usage data by disabling it in Settings.

---

## 6. License Revocation and Data Deletion

### 6.1 Self-Service Revocation
You can revoke your license at any time through the app:
- Go to Settings → License → Revoke License
- This removes the HWID binding from your device
- Allows activation on a different device

### 6.2 Data Deletion Timeline
After license revocation:
- HWID is immediately removed from active licenses
- Backup copies are deleted within 30 days
- Analytics data (anonymous) is retained indefinitely
